Glass Intensity Levels

Subtle Glass

Minimal transparency with very light blur. Perfect for readable overlays.

Backdrop blur: 4px
Opacity: 90%
Best for: Text-heavy content

Light Glass

Light transparency with moderate blur. Good balance of effect and visibility.

Backdrop blur: 8px
Opacity: 75%
Best for: Cards and panels

Medium Glass (Default)

Medium transparency with noticeable blur. The default glass effect.

Backdrop blur: 12px
Opacity: 60%
Best for: Hero sections

Heavy Glass

Heavy transparency with strong blur. Dramatic frosted glass effect.

Backdrop blur: 16px
Opacity: 40%
Best for: Overlays on images
